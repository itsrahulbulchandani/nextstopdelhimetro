Frequently Asked Questions

How does Next Stop work?

Next Stop uses your device's location services to track your position along Delhi Metro routes. When you're approaching your selected destination, the app sends you notifications, even when your phone is locked.

Does Next Stop work offline?

Yes! Once you've selected your route, Next Stop works completely offline. No internet connection is needed for the core alert functionality.

How much battery does Next Stop use?

Next Stop is optimized for battery efficiency. The app uses minimal battery power while tracking your journey, making it perfect for daily commutes.

Which Delhi Metro lines are supported?

Next Stop supports all Delhi Metro lines including Blue, Yellow, Pink, Red, Green, Violet, and more. Our database is regularly updated with the latest stations and routes.
